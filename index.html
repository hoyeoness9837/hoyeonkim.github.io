Why Did I Start PROGRAMMING?...

After spending 19 years in Korea, I decided to study abroad in the United States back in 2012. It was a new challenge I had to face as I had to learn a new language and adapt to a new culture. However, I faced these challenges with positive energy and eventually graduated from UCSB with a Bachelor’s Degree in Chemistry. After graduating, I realized that Chemistry is not the path I wanted to pursue -- rather I grew an interest in coding/programming. What made me fall in love with coding is that it provides direct feedback as soon as I’m typing on my keyboard. When I’m coding, it feels as if I’m spelling magic because any idea can become a reality.

Therefore, I made my decision to participate in a boot camp program to learn more about coding/programming. It was an intensive 12 weeks, Although It was a short time, I was able to learn the A to Z (scratching the surface) of web programming and cooperate with many other diligent people. Currently, I'm taking lower level language online classes to learn the fundamental CS knowledge and explore new technologies. I am looking forward to learning more and becoming a part of the problem-solving coding community.
